About Us

uComm Technologies & Education was created for the purpose of helping our customers improve their interactions with the online community through courses and digital technologies.

We love our planet and the ethnic diversity we find on it. That's why we serve our customers globally without distinction.

uComm Technologies & Education is firmly rooted in the principles of respect and good communication.

We are located in Greater Vancouver, B.C. (Canada). Our beautiful surroundings help us appreciate the need to care for our unique planet and its delicate environment.

We also have partners in Spain and Mexico. Our bilingual team can serve you in both English and Spanish, providing accurate translations for our services and products.

We hope to meet you in person or through one of our many ways to connect with you.
